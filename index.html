New Brawlers

Meet the exciting new characters that join the battle in v62.264

Alli

Mythic Assassin

A stealthy predator from Love Swamp with water-based combat and enrage-based abilities. Perfect for chasing down low-health enemies.

Trait: Enrage

Boosts movement speed when enemies are low on health for quick, deadly strikes.

Main Attack: Swamp Swipe

Dashes forward dealing damage. In bushes or water, leaps for a powerful blow scaling with movement speed.

Super Attack: Stalker

Gains pulsing stealth, reveals enemies, and deals damage based on target's max health.

Pro Tip: Use in maps with water or bushes. Save Super for picking off high-health targets late game.

Trunk

Epic Tank

A mechanical tree-like brawler powered by hyper-intelligent ants. Dominates with crowd control and buffs.

Main Attack: Ant Attack!

Spins in a circle dealing damage and scattering ants. Damage increases with distance from center.

Super Attack: Driven by Revenge

Dashes forward dropping ants along the way. Dash can bounce off walls for strategic area control.

Gadgets

Ants reduce enemy damage, provide vision, or heal Trunk depending on gadget used.

Pro Tip: Position ants in high-traffic areas. Pair with a healer for maximum durability.
